OUR    APPROACH

Websites with PURPOSE
Our design methodology centers around developing a deep understanding of how your new website will serve you. Through in-depth conversations and research into your work, we refine our designs to align with your goals.
Threads of Thought to a Tapestry of Ideas
We can help you pull together your vision and ideas into a coherent website that tells your story and invites users to step into your world. Our flexible approach means we can integrate into your vision, whether that means shaping initial concepts or, on the opposite end of the spectrum, executing fully realised wireframes.
Bespoke Made
We live in a time where off-the-shelf website builders and generic templates are everywhere, offering quick fixes but rarely providing a truly tailored solution. These one-size-fits-all options often fall short when it comes to reflecting your unique vision. Every website we create is bespoke, built from the ground up. Rather than relying on templates or cookie-cutter solutions, we craft a site that is uniquely tailored to your needs, ensuring both intuitive usability and a distinctive visual identity.
Specialists in Change
Whilst we are open to all clients, we are specialists in website design for social enterprises and charities with options for design approaches that are tailored for efficient, affordable design in small organisations.
Worthy Stories
Our Worthy Stories project is designed to help social enterprises and small charities have the website they never dreamed they could afford. We offer each corporate client, the opportunity to sponsor a worthy story website, providing subsidised design time to tell the stories of those enacting social good.
Regular Communication
We place a heavy focus on regular communication with our clients. In doing so, we create a feedback loop that greatly reduces the number of design iterations required translating into big savings in time and cost as well as website designs that accurately represent you and your cause.
Working to Budget
Unlike most website design companies, we don't believe in package pricing, but we do believe in budgets. This means if we set a budget with you and finish the site sooner than expected, we will only charge for the hours we spent. If you have a particularly tight budget we can also provide a set of minimum guaranteed requirements to complete in the budget as well as time dependent additional goals.
Future Proof Design
Once we have understood your goals and fully discussed the structure of the site and user experience we will begin to build the core site structure. We use variables for all elements of the site that allow us to make sweeping changes across the site if you change your mind about elements of the aesthetic later on. As your brand develops, this can allow you to easily adapt your sites aesthetic to match, future proofing your site. We fix our hourly rate for a minimum of 1 year from the completion of the site and commit to make all future changes at our hourly rate. This protects you from unexpected minimum charges or costs when you want to make changes or additions in future.
